Sarvangasana

Sarvangasana comes from Sarv meaning all, anga meaning body part and asana meaning posture. It is a shoulder stand, and its name indicates the influence on the functioning of different parts of the body. It is quite beneficial for the maintenance of mental as well as physical health.

Benefits

This posture exerts direct pressure on the pituitary gland which is essential for height growth. It improves the fresh blood supply to legs and abdomen as well as the whole circulatory system. The blood supply to the head region which includes eyes, nose, throat and ears is improved which improves their functioning. It also increases circulation of the pelvic and abdominal area, improves nose, ear and throat ailments.

It increases the lung capacity as the diaphragm needs to act against the gravity which helps in abdominal respiration and hence it is helpful in asthma treatment. Toxins in respiratory system are removed which improves the respiratory system.

It detoxifies the body due to the improvement of efficiency of body organs which leads to anti-ageing effects and youthfulness.

It normalizes the entire body weight due to the effect it causes on the thyroid.

It leads to increase in flexibility of neck with the toning of nerves passing across the neck.
