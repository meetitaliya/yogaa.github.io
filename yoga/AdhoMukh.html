Adho Mukha Svansana

AdhoMukhaSvansana is also known as Downward-Facing Dog which is among the most popular asanas in Yoga practices. It can be challenging for beginners initially, but with time it becomes one of the best ones for relaxation and strengthening of the body. For many reasons, this asana provides a wide range of benefits for the body which comprise of many physiological systems.
