Sun Salutation

Surya Namaskar or also known as Sun Salutation is quite an ancient technique which is meant to pay respect as well as express gratitude to the life-giving Sun. It is a great yoga practice which tones the entire body and helps in rejuvenating it. It strengthens the entire body and improves body posture.

Benefits

With 12 different asanas, it works wonders in increasing the height of the body as it stretches the entire body.

It improves the blood circulation. The active process of both inhalation and exhalation, the lungs get completely ventilated, and the blood remains oxygenated.

It detoxifies the body and helps in getting rid of excess toxic gases and carbon dioxide. If done in fast pace, it is a beneficial cardiovascular workout which stretches the muscles of the abdominal while reducing excess weight which is around the stomach simultaneously.

It tones arms, abs as well as give flexibility to the spine. It strengthens the complete skeletal system which also includes the ligaments.

It has anti-anxiety as well as calming properties. It helps in improving memory along with the nervous system.

It stabilizes the endocrine and thyroid gland activity.
