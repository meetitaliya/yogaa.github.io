Marjariasana

This asana is also known as cat pose which is derived from Marjari meaning cat and asana meaning pose. It is the movement which combines the forward bends along with back arches which gives the back the entire movement that it requires. The vertebrae become more mobile and the movements of thoracic, cervical and lumbar spine release entire tension which is trapped in them. The back and forth movements assist in circulation of spinal fluids which help in strengthening the spine. The limbs muscles are activated and provide the strength and energy in order to work better.

Benefits

In this asana, the spinal cord gets completely extended in both backward and forward directions which assist in increasing the height

It stretches, as well as strengthens along with the addition of flexibility to the spinal region.

It strengthens the shoulders and wrists.

It massages and activates the digestive organs and improves their processes.

It helps in toning the abdomen while assisting in getting rid of pockets of fat present unnecessarily.

It improves the oxygen and blood circulation in the entire body.

This stretch relaxes the mind and hence removes the strain and stress.
