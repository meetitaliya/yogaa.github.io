Gathiya Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon hot oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, ajwain, baking soda, salt, and hot oil.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a stiff dough. The dough should be smooth and non-sticky.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into cylindrical rolls.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  5. Using a gathiya maker or press, press out the dough through the sev disc directly into the hot oil to form thin strands.
  6. Fry the gathiya on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fried gathiya from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Allow the gathiya to cool down before storing them in an airtight container.
  9. Your crispy and savory Gathiya is ready to be enjoyed as a popular Indian snack!

Gathiya is a crunchy and delightful snack made from gram flour and is often served with tea or as a part of chaat.
