Delicious Burrito Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Large flour tortillas
  • Cooked rice (white or brown)
  • Canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • Cooked and seasoned meat (chicken, beef, pork, or tofu)
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.)
  • Sour cream, salsa, guacamole (optional, for toppings)
  • Chopped lettuce, diced tomatoes, diced onions (optional, for toppings)

Instructions:

  1. Warm the tortillas according to package instructions.
  2. Place a tortilla on a clean surface.
  3. Layer cooked rice, black beans, seasoned meat, and shredded cheese on the tortilla.
  4. Add any optional toppings like lettuce, tomatoes, onions, sour cream, salsa, or guacamole.
  5. Start rolling the tortilla from one side, folding in the edges as you go, to create a burrito.
  6. Continue rolling until the burrito is tightly wrapped.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining tortillas and ingredients.
  8. Serve the burritos immediately or wrap them in foil for a to-go option.
