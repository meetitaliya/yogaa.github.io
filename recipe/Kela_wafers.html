Kela (Banana) Wafers Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 raw bananas (firm and unripe)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Peel the raw bananas and place them in a bowl of water with a pinch of salt to prevent discoloration.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying. The oil should be hot but not smoking.
  3. Using a mandoline slicer or a knife, thinly slice the peeled bananas directly into the hot oil.
  4. Stir the banana slices gently to ensure they do not stick to each other.
  5. Fry the banana slices on medium heat until they turn crisp and golden brown.
  6. Remove the fried banana wafers from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Sprinkle salt over the hot wafers and toss gently to evenly coat them with salt.
  8. Allow the wafers to cool down before storing them in an airtight container.
  9. Your crispy and savory Kela (Banana) Wafers are ready to be enjoyed as a delicious snack!

Kela Wafers, also known as Banana Chips, are a popular South Indian snack made from thinly sliced raw bananas and deep-fried until crispy.
