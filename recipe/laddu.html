Laddu Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, etc.)
  • Raisins and edible gum (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat ghee over low heat.
  2. Add besan to the ghee and roast it on low to medium heat until it turns aromatic and changes color. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Remove from heat and let the roasted besan cool down slightly.
  4. Add powdered sugar, cardamom powder, chopped nuts, raisins, and edible gum if using. Mix well.
  5. While the mixture is still warm, shape it into small round laddus by rolling between your palms.
  6. Let the laddus cool completely and set before serving.
  7. Your delicious Laddus are ready to be enjoyed as a sweet treat!

Laddus are popular Indian sweets often prepared during festive occasions.
