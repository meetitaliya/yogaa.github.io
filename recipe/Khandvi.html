Khandvi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon green chili paste
  • Salt to taste
  • For Tempering:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • A pinch of asafoetida

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, water, turmeric powder, ginger paste, green chili paste, and salt. Whisk to form a smooth batter.
  2. Transfer the batter to a non-stick pan and cook over low heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens and becomes a thick paste. Make sure no lumps are formed.
  3. Quickly spread a thin layer of the batter onto a greased surface like the back of a thali or a countertop.
  4. Allow the spread batter to cool for a few minutes, then cut it into thin strips using a knife.
  5. Roll up the strips tightly and place them on a serving dish.
  6. Prepare the tempering by heating oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Let them splutter.
  7. Add asafoetida and pour the tempering over the rolled Khandvi.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and grated coconut if desired.
  9. Your delicate and delicious Khandvi is ready to be enjoyed as a popular Gujarati snack!

Khandvi is a unique and tasty snack made from gram flour, yogurt, and spices. Its soft and melt-in-the-mouth texture is a delight!
