Apple Halwa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium-sized apples, peeled, cored, and grated
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons raisins
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the grated apples and saut√© until they turn soft and most of the moisture evaporates.
  3. Add sugar and mix well. Cook until the sugar dissolves and the mixture thickens.
  4. Add cardamom powder, chopped nuts, and raisins. Mix well.
  5. If using saffron, dissolve it in a tablespoon of warm milk and add it to the halwa for color and flavor.
  6. Cook the halwa until it reaches a desired consistency and ghee starts to separate from the mixture.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.

Enjoy the delicious Apple Halwa warm or at room temperature as a delightful dessert!
