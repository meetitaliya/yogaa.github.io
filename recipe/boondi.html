Boondi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • 1/2 cup water (approx.)
  • Oil (for deep frying)
  • Salt to taste

For the Batter:

  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine 1 cup gram flour, turmeric powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Hold a perforated ladle (boondi ladle) over the hot oil and pour a spoonful of batter over it.
  4. Tap the ladle gently to let small drops of batter fall into the oil. Fry the boondis until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain excess oil.
  5. For the batter, mix 1/2 cup gram flour, red chili powder, cumin powder, asafoetida, and salt. Gradually add water to make a batter with pouring consistency.
  6. Dip the fried boondis into the batter, coating them well.
  7. Spread the coated boondis on a plate and allow them to dry and become crispy.
  8. Your delicious Boondi is ready to be enjoyed as a snack or as part of other recipes!

Feel free to add spices, nuts, or herbs to the boondi for added flavor.
