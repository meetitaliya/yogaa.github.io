Kulfi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup milk powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil. Let it simmer and reduce by about half, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add condensed milk, sugar, and milk powder to the simmering milk. Mix well and continue to cook until the mixture thickens.
  3. Stir in cardamom powder, chopped nuts, and saffron strands (soaked in a little milk).
  4. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool down to room temperature.
  5. Pour the cooled mixture into kulfi molds or small cups. You can insert wooden sticks or popsicle sticks into each kulfi.
  6. Place the molds in the freezer and let the kulfi set for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  7. To unmold the kulfi, briefly dip the molds in warm water to loosen them.
  8. Your delicious Kulfi is ready to be enjoyed as a cool and creamy dessert!

Kulfi is a popular Indian frozen dessert enjoyed by people of all ages.
