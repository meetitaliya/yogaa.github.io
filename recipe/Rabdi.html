Rabdi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring the milk to a boil. Let it simmer and reduce by about half, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  2. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the simmering milk. Mix well and continue to cook until the milk thickens further.
  3. Stir in chopped nuts and saffron strands (soaked in a little milk).
  4. Continue to cook and stir until the rabdi reaches a creamy and thick consistency.
  5. Remove from heat and let the rabdi cool down to room temperature.
  6. If desired, chill the rabdi in the refrigerator before serving.
  7. Your delectable Rabdi is ready to be enjoyed as a traditional Indian dessert!

Rabdi is often served on special occasions and celebrations as a rich and creamy sweet.
