Delicious Fried Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken pieces (legs, thighs, wings, or breast)
  • Buttermilk
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the buttermilk, salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Add the chicken pieces to the mixture, ensuring they are fully coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder to create the coating for the chicken.
  3. Remove the chicken from the buttermilk marinade and let any excess liquid drip off.
  4. Dredge each chicken piece in the seasoned flour mixture, making sure they are well coated. Shake off any excess flour.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot to about 350°F (175°C).
  6. Carefully place the coated chicken pieces into the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry for about 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Use a slotted spoon to remove the fried chicken from the oil and place it on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.
  8. Let the fried chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
