Perfect Bacon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Slices of bacon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil to catch any drippings and make cleanup easier.
  3. Place a wire rack on top of the lined baking sheet.
  4. Lay the bacon slices on the wire rack in a single layer, ensuring they don't overlap.
  5. Bake the bacon in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until it reaches your desired level of crispiness. Keep an eye on it, as cooking times can vary depending on the thickness of the bacon.
  6. Use tongs to transfer the cooked bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to absorb any excess grease.
  7. Allow the bacon to cool slightly before serving.
