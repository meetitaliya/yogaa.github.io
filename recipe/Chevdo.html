Chevdo (Chivda) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups thin poha (flattened rice)
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup roasted chana dal
  • 1/4 cup roasted split moong dal
  • 1/4 cup roasted cashews
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, place the thin poha and gently crush it with your hands to break any lumps.
  2. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add asafoetida, curry leaves, and slit green chilies. Saute for a minute.
  4. Add roasted peanuts, roasted chana dal, roasted split moong dal, roasted cashews, and raisins. Mix well.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix to coat the ingredients evenly with the spices.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and add the crushed poha to the pan. Mix well to combine.
  7. Continuously toss the mixture on low heat for a few minutes to ensure even roasting.
  8. Turn off the heat and let the chevdo cool down completely before storing it in an airtight container.
  9. Your delicious Chevdo (Chivda) is ready to be enjoyed as a crispy and savory snack!

Chevdo, also known as Chivda, is a popular Indian snack made from flattened rice, nuts, and spices.
