Puri Khaja Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Outer Layer (Puri):
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • For the Filling (Khaja):
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Puri Dough:
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, ghee, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Add water gradually and knead to form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for about 20-30 minutes.
  4. Prepare the Khaja Filling:
  5. In a separate mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and ghee. Mix to form a crumbly texture.
  6. Add cardamom powder and powdered sugar to the mixture. Mix well to form a sweet filling.
  7. Divide the Puri dough into small portions. Roll each portion into a thin circle (puri).
  8. Place a spoonful of the khaja filling in the center of each puri and fold it into a half-moon shape, sealing the edges.
  9. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan for frying.
  10. Fry the puri khajas on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  11. Remove from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  12. Your delicious Puri Khajas are ready to be enjoyed as a traditional Indian sweet!

Puri Khaja is a popular sweet snack often prepared during festivals and special occasions.
