Khaman Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Eno fruit salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • For Tempering:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • Sugar to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, yogurt, ginger paste, green chili paste, lemon juice, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Add water as needed to make a thick yet flowing batter.
  3. Grease a steamer plate or dish with oil.
  4. Add Eno fruit salt to the batter and mix well. The batter will become frothy.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased plate and steam in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the khaman comes out clean.
  6. Remove the khaman from the steamer and let it cool for a few minutes.
  7. Prepare the tempering by heating oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Let them splutter.
  8. Add sugar to the tempering and mix well.
  9. Pour the tempering over the khaman and cut it into squares.
  10. Your soft and spongy Khaman is ready to be enjoyed as a delightful snack or breakfast item!

Khaman is a popular Gujarati snack made from gram flour and is known for its fluffy texture and tangy-sweet flavor.
