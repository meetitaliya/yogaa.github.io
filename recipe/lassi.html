Lassi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Crushed ice (optional)
  • Chopped fresh fruits or mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine plain yogurt, cold water, sugar, salt, and cardamom powder.
  2. Blend until the mixture becomes smooth and frothy.
  3. If desired, add some crushed ice and blend again until well combined.
  4. Pour the lassi into glasses.
  5. Garnish with chopped fresh fruits or mint leaves, if using.
  6. Your refreshing Lassi is ready to be enjoyed as a cool and creamy drink!

Lassi is a popular Indian beverage that's perfect for quenching your thirst on a hot day.
