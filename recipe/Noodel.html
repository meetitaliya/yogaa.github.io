Simple Noodle Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Noodles of your choice (spaghetti, ramen, udon, etc.)
  • Vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, broccoli, etc.)
  • Protein (chicken, shrimp, tofu, etc.)
  • Sauce or broth (soy sauce, teriyaki, curry, etc.)
  • Optional toppings (green onions, sesame seeds, chili flakes, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the vegetables and protein by chopping or slicing them into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a pan, cook the protein until it's fully cooked. Set aside.
  4. In the same pan, saut√© the vegetables until they're tender and slightly crispy.
  5. Return the cooked protein to the pan and add your chosen sauce or broth. Stir to combine and heat through.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together to coat the noodles with the sauce and mix with the vegetables and protein.
  7. Once everything is heated through and well-mixed, remove from heat.
  8. Serve the noodles in bowls, and if desired, sprinkle with optional toppings.
