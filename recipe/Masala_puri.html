Masala Puri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Puri:
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying
  • For Masala:
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/4 cup boiled peas
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Sev for garnishing
  • Chopped green chilies and lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt for the puri.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a stiff dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into small balls.
  4. Roll out each ball into a thin puri using a rolling pin.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  6. Gently slide the rolled puri into the hot oil and fry until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  7. Remove the fried puris from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. In a mixing bowl, combine boiled and mashed potatoes, boiled peas, chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, chopped cilantro, chaat masala, red chili powder, cumin powder, amchur, roasted cumin seeds, and salt for the masala.
  9. To assemble, break the top of each puri and create a cavity for the masala.
  10. Fill each puri with the prepared masala mixture.
  11. Garnish with sev and chopped green chilies if desired.
  12. Squeeze some lemon juice over the masala if you prefer it tangy.
  13. Your tasty and tangy Masala Puri is ready to be enjoyed as a popular street food snack!

Masala Puri is a flavorful Indian street food snack made from crispy puris filled with a spiced potato mixture.
