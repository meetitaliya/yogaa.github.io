Mathiya Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, whole wheat flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, carom seeds, asafoetida, oil, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a firm and smooth dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls.
  4. Flatten each ball and use a fork or a mathiya maker to create a pattern on the surface.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  6. Gently slide the shaped mathiya into the hot oil and fry on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the fried mathiya from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  8. Allow the mathiya to cool down before storing them in an airtight container.
  9. Your crunchy and flavorful Mathiya is ready to be enjoyed as a tasty snack!

Mathiya is a traditional Gujarati snack made from gram flour and whole wheat flour, known for its unique texture and taste.
