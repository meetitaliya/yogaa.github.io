Fafda Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, asafoetida, ajwain, salt, and 2 tablespoons of oil.
  2. Gradually add water to the mixture and knead it into a stiff dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll each portion into a long strip on a greased surface using your palms.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  5. Gently lift the rolled dough strips and carefully place them in the hot oil. Fry on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fried fafdas from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Allow the fafdas to cool down and become crisp before serving.
  8. Your crunchy and savory Fafdas are ready to be enjoyed as a popular Gujarati snack!

Fafda is a crispy and flavorful snack that's often served with chutney or jalebi.
