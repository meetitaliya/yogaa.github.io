Delicious Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread (your choice of type)
  • Sliced deli meats (turkey, ham, roast beef, etc.)
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, Swiss, provolone, etc.)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional, for toppings)
  • Mayonnaise, mustard (optional, for condiments)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the slices of bread on a clean surface.
  2. Layer the deli meats and cheese slices on one slice of bread.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and any other desired toppings.
  4. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on the other slice of bread, if using.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Place the mayonnaise and mustard spread slice of bread on top of the toppings to create a sandwich.
  7. Cut the sandwich in half, if desired, and serve.
