Nankhatai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of baking soda
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, gram flour, powdered sugar, cardamom powder, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Add ghee to the mixture and knead everything together to form a smooth dough. The dough should be soft and pliable.
  3. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into round balls or flattened discs (nankhatai shape).
  5. Place the nankhatai on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Make sure to leave some space between each nankhatai.
  6. Gently press chopped nuts onto the top of each nankhatai for garnish.
  7. Bake the nankhatai in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes or until they turn golden brown at the edges.
  8. Remove from the oven and let the nankhatai cool down on the baking tray before transferring them to a wire rack.
  9. Your delicious Nankhatai are ready to be enjoyed as crispy and flavorful tea-time treats!

Nankhatai is a popular Indian shortbread cookie that's perfect for snacking.
