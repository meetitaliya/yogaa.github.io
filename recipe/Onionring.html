Delicious Onion Rings Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 large onions
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Peel and slice the onions into rings, separating the rings from each other.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, paprika, salt, and black pepper to create the dry coating mixture.
  3. Dip the onion rings into the buttermilk, allowing any excess to drip off.
  4. Coat the onion rings in the dry mixture, pressing the coating onto the rings to adhere.
  5. Place the coated onion rings on a baking sheet and let them rest for about 10-15 minutes. This helps the coating adhere better.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or pot to about 350°F (175°C).
  7. Carefully place a few coated onion rings into the hot oil at a time, without overcrowding the pan.
  8. Fry the onion rings for about 2-3 minutes per side or until they are golden brown and crispy.
  9. Use a slotted spoon to remove the fried onion rings from the oil and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.
  10. Serve the onion rings immediately as an appetizer or side dish.
