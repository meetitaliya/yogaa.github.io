Basic Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pasta of your choice (spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, etc.)
  • Pasta sauce (marinara, Alfredo, pesto, etc.)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Once cooked, carefully drain the pasta in a colander.
  4. In a separate pan, heat the pasta sauce over medium heat until warmed through.
  5. Add the drained pasta to the pan with the sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  6. Divide the sauced pasta into serving plates or bowls.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  8. Serve the pasta immediately as a comforting and satisfying meal.
