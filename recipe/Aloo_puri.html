Aloo Puri Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2-3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro (coriander leaves)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, amchur, asafoetida, chopped cilantro, oil, mashed potatoes, and salt.
  2. Knead the mixture into a firm yet pliable dough. Add water as needed to achieve the right consistency.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  4. Roll out each ball into a circular shape to form puris.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  6. Gently slide the rolled puri into the hot oil and press the top with a slotted spoon to help it puff up.
  7. Fry the puris on medium heat until they turn golden brown on both sides.
  8. Remove the fried puris from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  9. Your delicious Aloo Puri is ready to be served hot with chutney, curry, or yogurt!

Aloo Puri is a flavorful Indian dish made from deep-fried puris stuffed with a spiced potato filling.
