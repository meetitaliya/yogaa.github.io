Sohan Papdi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pan, heat ghee over low heat.
  2. Add besan to the ghee and roast it on low to medium heat until it turns aromatic and changes color. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat until the sugar dissolves and the mixture comes to a boil.
  4. Add the sugar syrup to the roasted besan, stirring continuously to combine. The mixture will thicken and leave the sides of the pan.
  5. Stir in cardamom powder and mix well.
  6. Pour the mixture onto a greased tray or plate and spread it evenly using a spatula.
  7. Sprinkle chopped nuts over the surface and press them gently with a spatula.
  8. Let the mixture cool down for a few minutes and then make cuts to form square or diamond shapes.
  9. Allow the Sohan Papdi to cool completely before removing the pieces from the tray.
  10. Your delicious Sohan Papdi is ready to be enjoyed as a crunchy and sweet treat!

Sohan Papdi is a popular Indian sweet that's often enjoyed during festivals and celebrations.
