Delicious Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the buttermilk, milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. It's okay if there are a few lumps.
  4. Preheat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Lightly grease the skillet with butter or cooking spray.
  6. For each pancake, pour about 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake and the edges look set, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Repeat the process for the remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed.
  9. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, butter, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
