Khir Mohan Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (cashews, almonds)
  • 1 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Once it boils, add lemon juice and stir gently until the milk curdles.
  2. Strain the curdled milk through a muslin cloth to separate the chenna (curdled milk solids) from the whey.
  3. Hang the chenna in the cloth for about 30 minutes to drain excess whey.
  4. Knead the drained chenna gently until it becomes smooth and soft.
  5. Divide the chenna into small portions and shape them into flat discs or patties.
  6. In a separate pan, dissolve sugar in a little water and bring it to a boil to make a sugar syrup. Add cardamom powder and saffron strands if using.
  7. Add the chenna patties to the simmering sugar syrup and let them soak for a few minutes.
  8. Heat ghee in a pan and lightly fry the chopped nuts until golden. Remove from heat and set aside.
  9. Remove the chenna patties from the sugar syrup and place them on a serving plate.
  10. Drizzle the remaining sugar syrup over the chenna patties and garnish with fried nuts.
  11. Your delightful Khir Mohan is ready to be enjoyed as a sweet treat!

Khir Mohan is a popular Bengali dessert that's perfect for celebrations.
