Kaju Katli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cashew nuts
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • Silver vark (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Grind the cashew nuts into a fine powder. You can use a food processor or grinder.
  2. In a non-stick pan, dissolve sugar in water over low heat to make a sugar syrup. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  3. Add the ground cashew powder to the sugar syrup and mix well to form a smooth mixture.
  4. Add cardamom powder and ghee. Continue cooking and stirring the mixture over low heat.
  5. As the mixture thickens, it will leave the sides of the pan. It should have a dough-like consistency.
  6. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool slightly. Knead it gently to form a smooth ball.
  7. Place the ball between two sheets of parchment paper and roll it out into a thin sheet using a rolling pin.
  8. Let it cool completely and set. If desired, apply silver vark on top for garnish.
  9. Cut the Kaju Katli into diamond-shaped pieces.
  10. Your delicious Kaju Katli is ready to be enjoyed as a traditional Indian sweet!

Kaju Katli is often served on special occasions and festivals.
