Sohan Halwa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (suji/rava)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a deep pan, heat ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add semolina and roast it in the ghee until it turns golden brown and aromatic. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. In a separate saucepan, combine sugar and water. Heat until the sugar dissolves and the mixture comes to a boil.
  4. Gradually add the sugar syrup to the roasted semolina, stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  5. Reduce the heat to low and add milk. Continue to cook and stir until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  6. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  7. Remove from heat and pour the halwa into a greased tray or plate. Smooth the top with a spatula.
  8. Sprinkle chopped nuts over the halwa and press them gently with a spatula.
  9. Let the halwa cool down completely before cutting it into pieces.
  10. Your rich and delicious Sohan Halwa is ready to be enjoyed as a traditional sweet!

Sohan Halwa is a popular Indian sweet known for its rich texture and delightful taste.
