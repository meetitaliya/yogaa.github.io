Sheera Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup semolina (suji/rava)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (cashews, almonds) for garnish
  • Raisins for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a deep pan, heat ghee over medium heat.
  2. Add semolina and roast it in the ghee until it turns golden brown and aromatic. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Meanwhile, in a separate saucepan, heat water and bring it to a boil.
  4. Gradually add the hot water to the roasted semolina, stirring continuously to prevent lumps from forming.
  5. Cook the mixture on low heat until the semolina absorbs the water and becomes soft and thick.
  6. Add sugar and cardamom powder to the mixture. Mix well and continue to cook until the sugar dissolves and the mixture thickens further.
  7. Remove from heat and let the sheera rest for a few minutes.
  8. While serving, garnish the sheera with chopped nuts and raisins.
  9. Your flavorful Sheera is ready to be enjoyed as a warm and comforting dessert!

Sheera is a popular Indian sweet made from semolina and is often prepared during festivals and special occasions.
