Gajab Paak Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup grated khoya (reduced milk)
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, etc.)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan over medium heat.
  2. Add besan to the ghee and roast it on low to medium heat until it turns golden brown and aromatic. Stir continuously to avoid burning.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the grated khoya. Mix well and cook for a few minutes until the khoya melts and blends with the besan.
  4. Add the powdered sugar and mix until it dissolves and combines with the mixture.
  5. Add chopped nuts and cardamom powder. Mix well.
  6. Continue cooking and stirring until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan. It should have a glossy texture.
  7. Remove from heat and quickly transfer the mixture to a greased tray or plate. Flatten it using a spatula.
  8. Let it cool down and set for a few hours.
  9. Once cooled and set, cut into desired shapes and serve your delicious Gajab Paak!

Gajab Paak is a delightful traditional sweet that can be enjoyed on special occasions and festivals.
