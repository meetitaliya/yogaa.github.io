Patras Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups colocasia leaves (taro leaves)
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup tamarind pulp
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin-coriander powder
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the colocasia leaves thoroughly and remove the stems.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, tamarind pulp, ginger paste, green chili paste, sugar, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin-coriander powder, asafoetida, oil, and salt.
  3. Add water gradually and mix to form a smooth batter with a thick consistency.
  4. Place a colocasia leaf with the veined side facing up. Spread a thin layer of the prepared batter on the leaf.
  5. Place another leaf on top, sandwiching the batter between the two leaves. Repeat this process with more leaves and batter.
  6. Roll the stack of leaves tightly and secure it with kitchen twine or thread.
  7. Steam the rolled colocasia leaves for about 20-25 minutes, or until the batter is cooked and the leaves are tender.
  8. Allow the steamed roll to cool down and then slice it into rounds.
  9. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Fry the sliced patras until they turn crispy and golden brown.
  10. Remove the fried patras from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  11. Your delicious and flavorful Patras are ready to be enjoyed as a snack or side dish!

Patras is a popular Indian snack made from colocasia leaves and gram flour, known for its unique taste and texture.
