Delicious Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on your pizza dough package.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone, baking sheet, or pizza pan.
  4. Spread a layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your preferred toppings, arranging them evenly over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle a little olive oil over the toppings and sprinkle with dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for the time indicated on your dough package or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
