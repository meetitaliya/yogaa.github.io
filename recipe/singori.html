Singori Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh khoya (milk solids)
  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Banana leaves or aluminum foil for wrapping

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine fresh khoya, grated coconut, powdered sugar, and cardamom powder. Mix well to form a smooth mixture.
  2. Cut banana leaves or aluminum foil into small squares for wrapping the singoris.
  3. Place a portion of the khoya-coconut mixture onto each leaf or foil square and fold it to form a parcel.
  4. Secure the parcels with twine or toothpicks.
  5. Heat a tawa (griddle) over medium heat. Place the singori parcels on the tawa and cook them, turning occasionally, until they are heated through and the banana leaves or foil slightly char.
  6. Remove from the tawa and carefully open the parcels.
  7. Your delightful Singoris are ready to be enjoyed as a unique and delicious sweet treat!

Singori is a traditional Uttarakhand dessert made with khoya, coconut, and wrapped in banana leaves.
