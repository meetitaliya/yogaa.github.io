Ras Malai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Ras Malai:
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese or paneer
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios) for garnish
  • Saffron strands for garnish (optional)
  • For the Ras (Milk Syrup):
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Ras Malai:
  2. Knead the ricotta cheese or paneer until smooth. Divide it into small portions and shape them into flattened balls.
  3. In a large pot, bring 2 cups of milk to a boil. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Gently slide the prepared cheese balls into the simmering milk. Cover and let them cook on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, or until they expand and become soft.
  5. Prepare the Ras (Milk Syrup):
  6. In a separate pot, bring 1 cup of milk to a boil. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Stir until the sugar dissolves.
  7. Assemble the Ras Malai:
  8. Remove the cooked cheese balls from the milk using a slotted spoon and place them in the prepared milk syrup. Let them soak for a few minutes.
  9. Chill the Ras Malai in the refrigerator for a few hours.
  10. Garnish with chopped nuts and saffron strands before serving.
  11. Your delightful Ras Malai is ready to be enjoyed as a rich and creamy Indian dessert!

Ras Malai is a popular Bengali sweet made from soft cheese balls soaked in milk syrup.
