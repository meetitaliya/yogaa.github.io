Arisa Pitha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons grated coconut
  • Oil (for deep frying)
  • 1 cup jaggery (or as per taste)
  • 1/2 cup water

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the syrup by combining jaggery and water in a saucepan. Heat until jaggery melts and syrup thickens. Set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine rice flour and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water to form a smooth batter. Add grated coconut and mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy. Drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  4. Dip the fried Arisa Pitha into the jaggery syrup. Allow it to soak for a minute or two.
  5. Remove from syrup and place on a plate. Let it cool down before serving.

Arisa Pitha is now ready to be enjoyed as a sweet and crispy treat! Serve fresh for the best flavor.
