Cheeseburger Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional, for toppings)
  • Cheese slices (cheddar, American, or your favorite)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (optional, for condiments)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix the ground beef, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Place the patties on the grill or pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness is reached.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, add a cheese slice to each patty and cover the pan to melt the cheese.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a cheese-covered patty on the bottom half of each bun.
  7. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments if desired.
  8. Top with the other half of the bun and serve.
