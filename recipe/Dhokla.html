Dhokla Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon green chili paste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Eno fruit salt
  • Salt to taste
  • For Tempering:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine besan, yogurt, ginger paste, green chili paste, turmeric powder, lemon juice, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Add water as needed to make a thick batter. Set aside for 10-15 minutes to let it rest.
  3. Grease a steamer plate or dish with oil.
  4. Add Eno fruit salt to the batter and mix well. The batter will become frothy.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased plate and steam in a steamer for about 15-20 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the dhokla comes out clean.
  6. Remove the dhokla from the steamer and let it cool for a few minutes.
  7. Prepare the tempering by heating oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, curry leaves, and green chilies. Let them splutter.
  8. Pour the tempering over the dhokla and cut it into squares or diamond shapes.
  9. Your fluffy and flavorful Dhokla is ready to be served as a delicious snack or breakfast item!

Dhokla is a popular Gujarati snack made from fermented gram flour and is known for its light and spongy texture.
