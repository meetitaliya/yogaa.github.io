Delicious Hotdog Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Hotdog buns
  • Hotdog sausages (beef, pork, chicken, or vegetarian)
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Chopped onions
  • Sauerkraut
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill, stovetop griddle, or pan over medium heat.
  2. Place the hotdog sausages on the grill or pan and cook, turning occasionally, until they are heated through and have grill marks.
  3. While the sausages are cooking, warm the hotdog buns by placing them on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Place a cooked sausage in each bun.
  5. Add your choice of toppings: mustard, ketchup, relish, chopped onions, sauerkraut, shredded cheese, and sliced jalapeños if desired.
  6. Serve the hotdogs immediately.
