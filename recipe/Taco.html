Delicious Taco Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Soft or hard taco shells
  • Ground beef, chicken, or tofu (cooked and seasoned)
  • Chopped lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, etc.)
  • Diced onions
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Sliced jalapeños (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Warm the taco shells according to package instructions.
  2. Place a taco shell on a clean surface.
  3. Layer cooked and seasoned meat, chopped lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, diced onions, and any other desired toppings.
  4. Add a dollop of sour cream, a spoonful of salsa, and a scoop of guacamole.
  5. If desired, add sliced jalapeños for an extra kick.
  6. Repeat the process for the remaining taco shells and ingredients.
  7. Serve the tacos immediately.
