Gota (Pakora) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/4 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon carom seeds (ajwain)
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon green chili paste
  • A handful of fresh spinach leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine gram flour, semolina, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, carom seeds, asafoetida, ginger paste, green chili paste, chopped spinach, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and mix to form a thick batter. The batter should coat the back of a spoon.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  4. Take spoonfuls of the batter and gently drop them into the hot oil to form small fritters.
  5. Fry the gotas on medium heat until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove the fried gotas from the oil and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  7. Your crispy and flavorful Gota (Pakora) is ready to be enjoyed as a popular Indian snack!

Gota, also known as Pakora, is a classic Indian snack made from gram flour and spices, often enjoyed with tea.
