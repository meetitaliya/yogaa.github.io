Jalebi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tablespoon gram flour (besan)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon yogurt
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • 1 cup warm water (approx.)
  • Oil or ghee (for deep frying)
  • For Sugar Syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, gram flour, baking soda, and yogurt.
  2. Add saffron strands to warm water and let them dissolve.
  3. Add the saffron water to the flour mixture and mix well. The batter should be smooth and thick. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, prepare the sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water and bringing it to a boil. Add cardamom powder and lemon juice. Simmer until the syrup has a slightly sticky consistency.
  5. Heat oil or ghee in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Pour the Jalebi batter into a squeeze bottle or a piping bag with a small round tip.
  7. Squeeze the batter into the hot oil in circular motions to form spirals. Fry until the Jalebis are golden and crispy, but not overly browned.
  8. Remove the fried Jalebis and immediately dip them into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for a minute or two.
  9. Take the Jalebis out of the syrup and serve warm as a sweet and indulgent treat!

Jalebi is a popular Indian dessert enjoyed on various occasions.
